## Accessibility

The status bar is **screen reader friendly**:

```html
<div class="status-bar" id="status-bar" role="status" aria-live="polite">
    Showing 47 of 523 images | 12 selected
</div>
```

*(Note: ARIA attributes can be added as enhancement)*

Screen readers will announce:
- "Status: Showing 47 of 523 images, 12 selected"
